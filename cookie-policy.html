Cookie Policy

This website, Travel Crown Blog, uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and remember your preferences. We value your privacy and keep cookie usage to the minimum necessary for the website to function properly.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve usability. Some cookies are essential for basic functions like language settings or remembering your session.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and localStorage to:

  • Remember your subscription entries.
  • Store your cookie consent preference so the banner doesn’t reappear.
  • Improve user experience without tracking your browsing behavior.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Currently, we do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. If third-party services are added in the future (for example, Google Analytics or embedded media), this policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how some features of the site work.

If you have any questions about our cookie policy, please contact us through the details provided on our Privacy Policy page.
