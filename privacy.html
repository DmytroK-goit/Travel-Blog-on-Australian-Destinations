Privacy Policy

At Travel Crown Blog, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect a minimal amount of information necessary for basic functionality. This may include:

  • Email addresses entered in the subscription form (stored locally in your browser’s localStorage).

We do not automatically collect personally identifiable data such as names, IP addresses, or browsing activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information stored locally (for example, your email for subscriptions) is used solely to enhance your experience with our site and is not transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All stored data remains in your browser’s local storage. You can delete it anytime through your browser’s settings or by clearing website data.

4. Third-Party Services

Currently, we do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking systems. If such services are added in the future, this policy will be updated to include details on what data may be shared and how it will be protected.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your local data at any time.
  • Refuse cookies or withdraw consent via your browser settings.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
