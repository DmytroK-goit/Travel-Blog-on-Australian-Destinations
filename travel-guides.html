Travel Guides

Explore Australia’s most captivating regions through the Crown Travel Guides. From sunlit harbours to misty forests and desert horizons — each guide combines history, culture, and practical advice for a memorable journey.

Sydney & Surrounds

Sydney offers a unique blend of old-world charm and coastal adventure. Stroll through The Rocks for a taste of colonial history, visit the Royal Botanic Garden for peaceful harbor views, and catch a ferry to Manly or Taronga Zoo for the perfect half-day escape.

Recommended routes include the Bondi to Coogee Walk, the Harbour Bridge Climb, and the Barangaroo Foreshore — each blending scenic beauty with a story of transformation.

Victoria Coastlines

The southern edge of Victoria reveals the continent’s wild artistry. The Great Ocean Road winds through seaside towns, limestone cliffs, and the majestic Twelve Apostles.

Stop at Lorne or Apollo Bay for local seafood, visit Otway National Park for rainforest trails, and explore Port Fairy for colonial heritage preserved in stone and story.

Tasmania Highlights

Tasmania feels like another world — a place where nature reigns and stories linger. Walk the Freycinet Peninsula for panoramic coastal views, discover Port Arthur Historic Site for a poignant look into Australia’s past, and explore Cradle Mountain for pure wilderness.

Every route here tells a tale — from the echoes of early settlers to the timeless rhythm of rivers and forests untouched by time.

Travel Tips

  • Respect heritage sites and local communities — preservation keeps the stories alive.
  • Australia’s climate varies widely. Always pack layers, sunscreen, and a hat.
  • Use public transport in cities and prepare well for remote journeys — signal coverage may vary.
  • Support local artisans, cafés, and eco-lodges to enrich both your experience and the community.
